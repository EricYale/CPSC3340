# Lab 3

## Wiring Description

### Joystick
- GND to ground
- 5V to 3V3 through resistor (around 100 kÎ©?)
- SW (switch) to pin 17
- Pull-up mode
- Normally HI

### Pushbutton
- Button straddles the "river" on the breadboard.
- Top right: GND
- Bottom right: 3V3 through resistor
- Bottom left: pin 4
- Pull-down mode
- Normally HI

### Switch
- One leg to pin 3
- One leg to GND
- Pull-down mode
